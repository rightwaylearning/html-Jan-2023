M S DHONI

MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI, born 7 July 1981, also known as MS Dhoni,is an Indian former international
cricketer who was the captain of Indian National Cricket team in limited-overs format from 2007-2017 and in test cricket 2008-2014.He is also a captain of CSK in ILP.
He led India to victory in three ICC trohies 2007 ICC World cup Twenty20, 2011 Cricket World cup and 2013 ICC Champions trophy, the most by any captain.

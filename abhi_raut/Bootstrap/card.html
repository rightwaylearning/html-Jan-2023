Suryakumar Yadav

Suryakumar Ashok Yadav (born 14 September 1990) is an Indian international cricketer. A right-handed batsman plays for Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League and for the Mumbai cricket team in Indian domestic cricket. He made his One Day International (ODI) debut for India on 18 July 2021 against Sri Lanka.[3] In November 2022, Yadav was ranked as the number one T20 International batsman.

Pune

Pune (Marathi: [puɳe] (listen); English: /ˈpuːnə/), also known as Poona, (the official name from 1818 until 1978) is one of the most important industrial and educational hubs of India, with an estimated population of 7.4 million as of 2020.[19][20][21][22] As of 2021, Pune Metropolitan Region is the largest in Maharashtra by area, with a geographical area of 7,256 sq km.[23] It has been ranked "the most liveable city in India" several times.[24] Pune is also considered to be the cultural and educational capital of Maharashtra.[25] Along with the municipal corporation area of PCMC, PMC and the three cantonment towns of Camp, Khadki, and Dehu Road, Pune forms the urban core of the eponymous Pune Metropolitan Region (PMR)
